Tip! the goal of the game is to fill a whole row/column/diagonal with your mark. Filling is done by clicking on a field on the 3x3 grid. The result is a draw if neither of the players achive their goals.

Tip! By clicking on the above buttons you can choose the type of game you want to enjoy. The first player always gets the mark 'X' and the other player gets the mark 'O'.
